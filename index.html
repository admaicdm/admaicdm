About the Conference

Welcome to the ADMA Annual Conference 2026, hosted by the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. This conference brings together leading researchers, academics, and practitioners in discrete mathematics and its applications from around the world.

The Academy of Discrete Mathematics and Applications (ADMA) is dedicated to promoting research and education in discrete mathematics, combinatorics, graph theory, and related fields. This annual conference serves as a platform for sharing cutting-edge research, fostering collaborations, and advancing the field.

About IIT Madras

The Indian Institute of Technology Madras is one of India's premier engineering and technology institutions, consistently ranked as the #1 institute in the country [web:2][web:42]. Established in 1959, IIT Madras is a residential institute spread across 700 acres of lush greenery, which was formerly part of the adjoining Guindy National Park [web:47].

The campus is home to nearly 750 faculty members, 14,000 students, and state-of-the-art facilities including 100+ laboratories, modern classrooms, an Olympic-standard sports complex, and a world-class central library [web:42]. Located in Chennai, the campus is conveniently situated 10 km from Chennai Airport and 12 km from Chennai Central Railway station [web:2].
